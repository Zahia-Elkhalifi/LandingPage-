Our services

You’ll enjoy knowing our dedicated team will do whatever is needed to keep your pets happy, healthy

safe when you’re away from home.


Puppy Sitting

You love your new puppy with all your hear but you’re less fond of stained carpets, chewed up shoes, and slobbery clothing. Our local sitters can help you customize the perfect feeding for pup while helping them learn the proper way to behave in home.

Dog Walking

Most dogs can’t wait to hear W-A-L-K, but it isn’t always easy to find time for their favorite activity. We can help you give them their daily dose of fun-filled exercise with a 30, 45, or 60-minute walk that includes any feeding, watering, or potty breaks they need.

Pet Sitting

Whether you have a dog, cat, bird, fish, gerbil, snake, or really any other type of pet, you can trust us to look after them while you’re away. We’ll take care of any food, water, waste, and exercise needs, and, of course, give your pet all the love.

Overnight Pet Care

We offer overnight or “almost” overnight pet sitting services. You can breathe a sigh of relief knowing your pet will get to maintain their normal routine of feeding, playtime, and exercise in the safety and comfort of your household.

Pet Taxi

Your pet leads a busy life; one you can’t always keep up with. But we’re here to help. Whether it be a nearby vet appointment, trip to the groomers, or playdate at the park, our pet taxi service can make sure your pet safely gets where they need to go.

Medical Administration

If your pet has a strict schedule for taking prescribed medication, enlisting a little help is never a bad idea. Our administrators are experienced with pills, injections, and fluids and can ensure that your pet is getting the right medication at the right time.
